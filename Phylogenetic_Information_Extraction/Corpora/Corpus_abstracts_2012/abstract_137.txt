The outbreak of viral meningitis in Linhai county in 2004 was caused by the two classes of E30 strains with G and H genotype existed simultaneously. The Linhai E30 strains had maximum genetic relations to the Zhejiang, Jiangsu and Shangdong strains of E30. The H genotype was inferred to be a new variant strain, which was first isolated in Zhejiang province in 2002.
