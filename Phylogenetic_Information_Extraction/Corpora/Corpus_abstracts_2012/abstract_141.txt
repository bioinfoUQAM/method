The HIV subtype strains detected in those HIV infected marriage-based immigrant women in the rural area of Zhejiang province characterized with diversity, showing CRF08_BC and CRF01_AE were the main HIV strain subtypes. HIV infection originated mainly from Yunnan province and nearby regions. Heterosexual behaviors of the marriage-based immigrant women in the original region where they had their residence registration, seemed to be the primary high risk factors for these women. Surveillance and intervention programs on these marriage-based immigrant women and their family members should be improved.
